Who Was Dokka Seethamma?

Dokka Seethamma was one of Andhra Pradesh's most respected philanthropists. She became famous for serving food to travelers, the poor, and anyone who arrived at her doorstep with hunger. She believed that kindness begins with feeding those in need.

"A hungry person should never leave without food."

The Kitchen of Kindness

🍚 Food

Every meal was prepared with care and served with dignity.

🤝 Compassion

Every visitor was welcomed equally, regardless of their background.

❤️ Humanity

Her greatest wealth was the happiness of those she helped.

Her Legacy Lives On

Today, Dokka Seethamma is remembered as a symbol of compassion and selfless service. Her life continues to inspire people across Andhra Pradesh and beyond.

"Kindness is remembered long after a meal is forgotten."
