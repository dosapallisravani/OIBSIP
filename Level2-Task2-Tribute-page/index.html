Her Inspiring Story

Dokka Seethamma was a legendary philanthropist from Andhra Pradesh. Her kindness and generosity became an inspiration for generations. She believed that no person should leave hungry.

With love and dedication, she served food to countless travelers and needy people, turning her home into a place of humanity.
